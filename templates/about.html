About Voice Emotion Detection

Voice Emotion Detection is an AI-powered system designed to analyze human speech and identify emotional states such as happy, sad, angry, neutral, and confident. The system operates fully offline and processes real-time audio securely.

Real-Time Voice Capture

Uses live microphone input to capture speech signals continuously, ensuring natural and spontaneous emotion detection.

Deep Learning Intelligence

Powered by HuBERT and Wav2Vec2-based neural networks trained for emotion recognition from speech patterns.

Offline & Secure

All predictions are performed locally without sending audio data to external servers, ensuring privacy and security.

Confidence Scoring

Along with emotion labels, the system provides a confidence percentage to indicate prediction reliability.
